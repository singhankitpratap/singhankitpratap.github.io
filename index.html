About me

Hello! I am Ankit Pratap Singh. I am a PhD student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, advised by Professor Namrata Vaswani. My research involves developing robust machine learning, Compressive Sensing, and Reinforcement Learning (RL) algorithms in the face of Byzantine failures. By leveraging Signal Processing, High Dimensional Probability and Linear Algebra, I aim to provide convergence guarantees, ensuring these algorithms perform accurately despite adversarial conditions. Additionally, I simulate these algorithms in TensorFlow, PyTorch to validate their robustness and efficiency under diverse scenarios. I am continually seeking and open to collaborations on exciting projects.
