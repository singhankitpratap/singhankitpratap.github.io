About me

Hello! I am Ankit Pratap Singh. I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Iowa State University, advised by Professor Namrata Vaswani. My research interests lie in federated machine learning, information theory, reinforcement learning, and compressive sensing. Key contributions include developing a Byzantine-resilient federated few-shot learning algorithm, which uses low-dimensional representation for efficient learning from minimal data. I have also created a novel technique called Subspace Median, which enables Principal Component Analysis (PCA) on distributed or federated datasets, even with up to 50% of the devices being corrupt. I'm proficient in simulating machine learning models using TensorFlow and PyTorch and have hands-on experience federating these models with Docker Swarm and deploying scalable solutions on AWS. I am continually seeking and open to collaborations on exciting projects.
